Welcome to jerry fitness center

At jerry fitness center, we believe in empowering you to reach your fitness goals. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned athlete, our state-of-the-art facility and experienced trainers are here to support you every step of the way.


Our Services : )

Group Classes:

Join our fun and energetic group classes, including yoga, pilates, Zumba, spinning, and more. These classes are designed to keep you motivated and make working out enjoyable.


Nutrition Counseling:

Achieve your fitness goals faster with the help of our professional nutritionists. They will create a personalized meal plan that complements your workout routine and promotes a healthy lifestyle.


Cardio and Strength Equipment:

Our gym is equipped with the latest cardio and strength training equipment to ensure you have everything you need for a complete workout.


Membership Plans

Basic Membership:

Access to all gym equipment and facilities, including locker rooms and showers.


Premium Membership:

Includes all Basic Membership benefits plus unlimited group classes and one personal training session per month.
